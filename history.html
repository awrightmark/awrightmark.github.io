The Evolutionary History of the Human Body

What creates the human experience?

How do we perceive color and light? Why do we walk on two feet, and not three or four? What causes disease or spurs feelings of pleasure or fear?

Evolutionary biology provides us with the toolkit to uncover the Why question of the human experience. There are finite ways a biological system can be organized according to the laws of physics and chemistry, and finite ways biological systems can emerge over time. A mouse will never give birth to an elephant.

Here, I start from the beginning of it all and document the origin and evolution of every aspect of the anatomically modern human body, according to current research.
