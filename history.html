Functional Morphologist ⚬ Paleontologist ⚬ Data Scientist

What creates the human experience?

How do we perceive color and light? Why do we walk on two feet, and not three or four? What causes disease or spurs feelings of pleasure or fear?

To understand our bodies and why they work the way they do, we must start from the beginning of it all. Evolutionary biology is ultimately a historical science, and from it we can learn much about why we have the experiences we do today.
